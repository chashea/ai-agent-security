# Contoso Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Last Updated: 2026-01-15**
**Classification: Internal**
**Owner: Finance Operations**

---

## Overview

This policy governs the reimbursement of business expenses incurred by Contoso employees. All expenses must be legitimate, reasonable, and incurred in the performance of company business. Employees are expected to exercise the same care in incurring expenses as they would with their own funds.

---

## Approval Thresholds

| Expense Amount | Required Approver |
|---|---|
| Up to $500 | Direct Manager |
| $501 – $5,000 | Director or above |
| $5,001 – $25,000 | Vice President or above |
| Above $25,000 | CFO approval required |

All approvals must be obtained prior to incurring the expense when possible. Retroactive approvals require written justification.

---

## Per Diem Rates

Per diem covers meals and incidental expenses (M&IE) when overnight travel is required.

| Location Type | Daily Rate |
|---|---|
| Domestic – Standard City | $75/day |
| Domestic – High-Cost City (NYC, SF, DC, Boston) | $110/day |
| International – Standard | $95/day |
| International – High-Cost (London, Zurich, Tokyo) | $140/day |

Per diem is reduced by 25% for partial travel days (departure or return day). Employees may not claim per diem and also submit individual meal receipts.

---

## Travel Policy

### Air Travel
- Book economy class for all domestic flights under 6 hours.
- Business class is permitted for international flights over 6 hours with Director approval.
- All flights must be booked at least 14 days in advance unless an exception is approved.
- Use Contoso's preferred booking tool (Concur) for all air travel.

### Hotel
- Book standard business-class accommodations. Suite upgrades require VP approval.
- Maximum nightly rate: $250 domestic, $350 international (excluding taxes).
- Use Contoso preferred hotel vendors when available for negotiated rates.

### Ground Transportation
- Use rideshare (Uber Business, Lyft Business) or public transit where practical.
- Personal vehicle mileage reimbursed at the current IRS standard rate ($0.70/mile as of 2026).
- Car rentals require manager approval and must use economy or compact class.

---

## Receipt Requirements

- Receipts are required for all individual expenses $25 or greater.
- Receipts must show vendor name, date, itemized charges, and amount paid.
- Receipts must be submitted within 30 days of incurring the expense.
- Lost receipts require a signed Lost Receipt Affidavit and manager approval.

---

## Corporate Card Usage

- Corporate cards are issued to employees who travel more than 4 times per year.
- Cards must be used only for legitimate business expenses.
- Monthly statements must be reconciled and submitted within 5 business days of cycle close.
- Personal charges on corporate cards are a policy violation and may result in disciplinary action.

---

## Prohibited Expenses

The following are not reimbursable under any circumstances:

- Alcoholic beverages (except client entertainment with manager pre-approval, capped at $50/person)
- Personal entertainment, gym memberships, or subscription services
- Spouse or family member travel unless pre-approved as part of a recognized company event
- Fines, penalties, or traffic tickets
- Expenses incurred more than 90 days prior to submission
- First-class domestic airfare
- Minibar charges
- In-flight Wi-Fi on flights under 3 hours

---

## Submission Process

1. Log into Concur at `concur.contoso.internal`.
2. Create a new expense report and attach receipts (photos or scans accepted).
3. Tag each line item with the correct cost center and GL code.
4. Submit for manager approval.
5. Approved reports are processed on the 15th and last business day of each month.
6. Reimbursement is deposited directly to the employee's payroll account.

---

## Policy Violations

Submitting false, inflated, or unsupported expense claims constitutes fraud and may result in termination and legal action. Questions about policy interpretation should be directed to `finance-ops@contoso.com`.
