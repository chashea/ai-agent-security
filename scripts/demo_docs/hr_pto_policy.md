# Contoso PTO and Leave Policy

**Classification: Internal**
**Last Updated: 2026-01-15**
**Document Owner: People & Culture**
**Policy Number: HR-201**

---

## Overview

Contoso provides a comprehensive leave program designed to support employees' personal, family, and civic needs. All leave types described in this document apply to full-time employees. Part-time employees (20–39 hours per week) accrue PTO at 50% of the rates listed below. Contractors and temporary workers are not eligible for these benefits.

---

## Paid Time Off (PTO) Accrual

PTO covers vacation, personal days, and short-duration personal illness (see Sick Leave section for extended illness).

**Accrual rates (based on years of service at Contoso):**

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Accrual | Accrual Per Pay Period (biweekly) |
|---|---|---|
| 0–2 years | 15 days (120 hours) | 4.62 hours |
| 3–5 years | 20 days (160 hours) | 6.15 hours |
| 6–9 years | 25 days (200 hours) | 7.69 hours |
| 10+ years | 30 days (240 hours) | 9.23 hours |

**PTO rules:**
- PTO begins accruing on day one of employment
- Employees may not use PTO before it is accrued (no negative balances)
- Maximum accrual cap is 1.5x the annual accrual rate (e.g., 22.5 days cap for new employees in their first two years)
- At separation, accrued and unused PTO is paid out in the final paycheck, subject to applicable state law

**Requesting PTO:** Employees must submit PTO requests in Workday with a minimum of two weeks' notice for requests of three or more consecutive days. Shorter requests are subject to manager approval based on business needs.

## Company Holidays

Contoso observes the following paid holidays each calendar year. When a holiday falls on a Saturday, it is observed on the preceding Friday. When it falls on a Sunday, it is observed on the following Monday.

1. New Year's Day — January 1
2. Martin Luther King Jr. Day — third Monday in January
3. Presidents' Day — third Monday in February
4. Memorial Day — last Monday in May
5. Juneteenth — June 19
6. Independence Day — July 4
7. Labor Day — first Monday in September
8. Thanksgiving Day — fourth Thursday in November
9. Day After Thanksgiving — fourth Friday in November
10. Christmas Eve — December 24
11. Christmas Day — December 25

In addition, Contoso provides one floating holiday per calendar year. The floating holiday must be used within the calendar year and does not carry over.

## Sick Leave

Contoso provides a separate sick leave bank, distinct from PTO, for absences due to employee illness, medical appointments, or care of a seriously ill immediate family member.

- **Accrual:** 1 day (8 hours) per month, up to a maximum balance of 60 days (480 hours)
- Sick leave does not pay out at separation
- Sick leave may be used in conjunction with FMLA (see below)
- Employees who exhaust sick leave and PTO balances during an extended illness should contact HR to discuss short-term disability eligibility

---

## Parental Leave

**Birth parent:** 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave following the birth of a child, to be taken within 12 months of the birth.

**Non-birth parent / adoption / foster placement:** 8 weeks of fully paid parental leave, to be taken within 12 months of the qualifying event.

- Parental leave runs concurrently with FMLA where applicable
- Employees must provide HR with at least 30 days advance notice where practicable
- Parental leave is available to employees who have completed at least 90 days of employment

---

## Family and Medical Leave (FMLA)

Contoso complies with the federal Family and Medical Leave Act. Eligible employees (12+ months of employment, 1,250+ hours in the past 12 months) may take up to 12 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave per year for qualifying reasons, including:

- Birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child
- Serious health condition of the employee or an immediate family member
- Qualifying military exigencies

Employees are required to use accrued PTO and sick leave concurrently with FMLA leave. FMLA paperwork must be submitted to HR within 15 days of the leave start date.

---

## Bereavement Leave

Contoso provides paid bereavement leave for the loss of an immediate or extended family member.

| Relationship | Paid Days |
|---|---|
| Spouse, domestic partner, child, stepchild | 5 days |
| Parent, stepparent, sibling, grandparent | 3 days |
| In-laws (parent, sibling), grandchild | 2 days |
| Close friend or colleague (at manager discretion) | 1 day |

Bereavement leave must be taken within 30 days of the date of passing. Additional time off may be arranged as PTO in coordination with your manager.

---

## Jury Duty and Civic Leave

Employees summoned for jury duty are entitled to paid civic leave for the duration of their service, up to 15 business days per calendar year. Contoso will pay the difference between jury duty pay received and the employee's regular daily rate.

For civic leave beyond 15 days, employees may use PTO or request an unpaid leave of absence.

Employees must provide a copy of the jury summons to their manager and HR as soon as it is received and are expected to return to work on any day they are released before 3:00 PM local time.

---

## Unpaid Leave of Absence

At Contoso's discretion, employees may request an unpaid leave of absence for personal reasons not covered by other leave types. Requests must be submitted in writing to HR at least 30 days in advance. Approval is not guaranteed and depends on business needs and the employee's standing.
