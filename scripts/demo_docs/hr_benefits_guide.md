**Classification: Internal**
**Last Updated: 2026-01-15**
**Document Owner: Total Rewards / People & Culture**

## Overview

Contoso offers a competitive benefits package to full-time employees. Benefits enrollment opens annually during the Open Enrollment period in November, with coverage effective January 1 of the following year. New hires have 30 days from their start date to enroll. Elections not made within this window default to the minimum plan tier (Bronze) for medical and waive all other voluntary benefits.

Benefits questions should be directed to benefits@contoso.com or the Benefits Hotline at ext. 5300.

---

## Health Insurance

Contoso offers three medical plan tiers through BlueCross BlueShield. All plans include preventive care at no cost when using in-network providers.

### Bronze Plan
- **Monthly employee premium:** $85 (individual) / $220 (family)
- **Annual deductible:** $2,500 individual / $5,000 family
- **Out-of-pocket maximum:** $7,000 individual / $14,000 family
- **Coinsurance after deductible:** 30% employee / 70% plan
- **Primary care copay:** $30 after deductible
- **Specialist copay:** $60 after deductible
- Compatible with an HSA (see below)

### Silver Plan
- **Monthly employee premium:** $145 (individual) / $375 (family)
- **Annual deductible:** $1,000 individual / $2,000 family
- **Out-of-pocket maximum:** $4,500 individual / $9,000 family
- **Coinsurance after deductible:** 20% employee / 80% plan
- **Primary care copay:** $20 after deductible
- **Specialist copay:** $45 after deductible
- Compatible with an HSA

### Gold Plan
- **Monthly employee premium:** $210 (individual) / $540 (family)
- **Annual deductible:** $350 individual / $700 family
- **Out-of-pocket maximum:** $2,500 individual / $5,000 family
- **Coinsurance after deductible:** 10% employee / 90% plan
- **Primary care copay:** $15 (no deductible requirement)
- **Specialist copay:** $35 (no deductible requirement)
- Not HSA-compatible (qualifies for FSA instead)

**Dental:** Contoso offers dental coverage through Delta Dental. Employee premium is $12/month (individual) or $32/month (family). Plan covers 100% of preventive care, 80% of basic restorative, and 50% of major restorative up to a $1,500 annual maximum.

**Vision:** Vision coverage through VSP. Employee premium is $6/month (individual) or $14/month (family). Covers one annual exam and $150 toward frames or contact lenses per year.

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

Contoso's 401(k) plan is administered by Fidelity Investments.

- **Employee contribution:** Up to IRS limit ($23,500 for 2026; $31,000 if age 50+)
- **Contoso match:** 100% match on the first 4% of eligible compensation contributed, plus 50% match on the next 2% — maximum effective match of 5% of eligible compensation
- **Vesting schedule:** Employer match vests on a 3-year graded schedule:
  - Year 1: 0%
  - Year 2: 33%
  - Year 3: 67%
  - Year 4+: 100%
- Employees are auto-enrolled at 3% contribution upon hire; opt-out is available within 90 days
- Roth 401(k) option is available alongside traditional pre-tax contributions
- Loans against the 401(k) balance are permitted under IRS rules; contact Fidelity at fidelity.com/contoso or 1-800-555-0240

## Health Savings Account (HSA)

Employees enrolled in the Bronze or Silver plan are eligible for a tax-advantaged HSA through Optum Bank.

- **2026 IRS contribution limits:** $4,300 (individual) / $8,550 (family); additional $1,000 catch-up for age 55+
- **Contoso annual HSA contribution:** $500 (individual) or $1,000 (family), deposited in January
- Funds roll over year to year with no expiration
- After age 65, funds may be withdrawn for any purpose without penalty (income tax applies for non-medical use)

---

## Flexible Spending Account (FSA)

Employees enrolled in the Gold plan (or who waive medical coverage) are eligible for an FSA through WEX Health.

- **Healthcare FSA limit:** $3,300 per year (2026 IRS limit)
- **Dependent Care FSA limit:** $5,000 per year (IRS limit)
- Use-it-or-lose-it: up to $660 (20%) may roll over to the following year; remainder is forfeited
- FSA debit card provided for eligible expenses

## Life and Disability Insurance

**Basic Life Insurance:**
Contoso provides basic life insurance at no cost to the employee equal to 2x annual base salary, up to a maximum benefit of $500,000. Coverage is provided through MetLife.

**Supplemental Life Insurance:**
Employees may elect supplemental coverage in increments of 1x salary, up to 5x salary, at employee cost. Rates vary by age group and are available during Open Enrollment.

**Spouse/Domestic Partner and Child Life Insurance:**
Supplemental coverage available for dependents: up to $100,000 for spouse/partner and $10,000 per child.

**Short-Term Disability (STD):**
Contoso-paid. Provides 70% of base salary for up to 12 weeks following a 7-day elimination period. Coordinates with paid sick leave.

**Long-Term Disability (LTD):**
Contoso-paid. Provides 60% of base salary beginning after the STD period ends, up to Social Security Normal Retirement Age. Includes own-occupation definition of disability for the first 24 months.

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

All employees and household members have access to Contoso's EAP through LifeWorks, at no cost.

- **Counseling:** Up to 8 free confidential counseling sessions per issue per year (mental health, stress, grief, relationship issues)
- **Legal consultations:** Free 30-minute consultation per legal matter; referrals to attorneys at reduced rates
- **Financial counseling:** Free consultations with certified financial counselors
- **Work-life services:** Childcare and elder care referrals, wellness resources
- **Access:** 1-800-555-0178 (24/7) or lifeworks.com/contoso

The EAP is completely confidential. Contoso does not receive any individually identifiable information from LifeWorks.

## Tuition Reimbursement

Contoso supports continued education through its Tuition Reimbursement Program.

- **Annual maximum:** $5,250 per calendar year (the IRS tax-free limit; reimbursements above this amount are taxable)
- **Eligibility:** Full-time employees who have completed at least one year of service
- **Qualifying expenses:** Tuition, required course fees, and textbooks at accredited colleges, universities, or vocational schools
- **Grade requirement:** Minimum grade of B (or equivalent) for reimbursement
- **Process:** Submit pre-approval form to HR before the semester starts; submit receipts and grade documentation within 60 days of course completion
- **Clawback:** Employees who voluntarily separate within 12 months of receiving reimbursement must repay 100% of the amount; within 24 months, 50%

Courses do not need to be directly related to the employee's current role but must contribute to professional development or career advancement.

## Additional Benefits

**Commuter Benefits:**
Pre-tax transit and parking accounts through WageWorks. IRS 2026 monthly limits: $325 for transit passes, $325 for parking.

**Employee Discounts:**
Access to the Contoso Perks portal for discounts on travel, entertainment, electronics, fitness memberships, and more.

**Fitness Reimbursement:**
Up to $300 per calendar year reimbursed for gym memberships, fitness classes, or eligible wellness apps. Submit receipts to benefits@contoso.com.

**Paid Volunteer Time:**
Employees receive 16 hours (2 days) of paid volunteer time per year to participate in community service activities. Submit through Workday under "Community Time."
